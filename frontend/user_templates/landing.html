About McMaster Seat Tracker

McMaster Seat Tracker is a free course availability monitoring service designed specifically for McMaster University students. Our automated system checks Mosaic (McMaster's course registration system) continuously and sends you instant email alerts when seats become available in your desired courses.

Why Use a Course Seat Tracker?

McMaster courses fill up quickly during registration periods. Instead of manually refreshing Mosaic every few minutes, our seat tracker monitors course availability automatically and notifies you immediately when a spot opens. This gives you the best chance to register for high-demand courses before they fill up again.

Features for McMaster Students

  • Real-time monitoring: Continuous checks of McMaster Mosaic course availability
  • Instant email alerts: Get notified within seconds when a course opens
  • Multiple term support: Track courses across Winter, Spring, and Fall terms
  • Easy course selection: Search and select from all McMaster subjects
  • Free service: No subscription fees or hidden costs

How to Track McMaster Course Seats

Simply create a free account, add the courses you want to track (subject code, course number, and term), and we'll monitor Mosaic for seat availability. When a seat becomes available, you'll receive an email notification with a direct link to register in Mosaic.
